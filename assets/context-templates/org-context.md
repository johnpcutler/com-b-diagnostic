# Organization Context Template

> This is a template. Copy this file to `user-context/` at the root of your workspace (for example, `user-context/org-context.md`), then fill it out.
> You can rename the copied file for your environment (for example, `org-context-enterprise.md`).
> If a section is unknown, leave it blank rather than guessing. The agent should treat this as background assumptions to refine, not fixed truth.

## Macro-level incentives

- What is formally rewarded (promotion criteria, recognition, bonus signals)?
- What do people actually optimize for in day-to-day work?
- Where are there clear incentive misalignments?

## Stated vs actual culture

- What culture does leadership describe publicly?
- What behaviors are actually tolerated or punished?
- How are speed, quality, failure, and dissent handled in practice?

## Organizational structure (silos, hierarchy)

- How is work divided across functions or business units?
- Where are the key handoffs, approval gates, and escalation paths?
- Which teams are interdependent but poorly aligned?

## Strategic priorities

- What are the official top priorities this cycle?
- Where is budget, staffing, and leadership attention actually going?
- Which priorities repeatedly displace longer-term work?

## General risk appetite

- What kinds of risk are acceptable (delivery, quality, compliance, reputational)?
- How are experiments approved and how much failure is tolerated?
- Where do risk controls slow behavior change, and where are controls weak?
